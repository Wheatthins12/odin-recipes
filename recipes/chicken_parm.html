Chicken Parmesan

This should be how your finished Chicken Parm looks!

Chicken Parm is a very popular Italian dish. It consists of chicken breast, marinara sauce and your choice of pasta! It is very customizable, in the sense that you can choose the sauce or pasta to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • Marinara Sauce or Sauce of your liking
  • Pasta of your choice, fettuccini goes great!
